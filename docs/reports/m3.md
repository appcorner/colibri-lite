## Memory evidence

The tiny fixture has 2 layers, 2 KV heads, head dimension 4, F32 values, and a
maximum context of 32 positions. Its fixed KV allocation is:

```text
2 layers * 32 positions * 2 KV heads * 4 values * 2 (K+V) * 4 bytes
= 4,096 bytes
```

- Key/value `Vec` capacities are captured before prefill and remain unchanged
  after every decode through position 32.
- Cache byte size remains exactly 4,096 bytes throughout the full-context run.
- Decode at position 33 is rejected without changing cache or sequence state.
- Repeated cache create/append/drop coverage passes.
- Streaming full-context decode uses a 9,216-byte expert budget; resident and
  peak expert bytes remain at or below that budget while loads and bytes-read
  metrics remain nonzero.

`KvCache::byte_size` accounts for F32 key/value elements and intentionally
excludes small `Vec` and Rust-structure metadata.

## Scope review

- No third-party Rust dependency was added.
- No unsafe code, SIMD, FFI, quantization, memory mapping, GPU backend,
  tokenizer, server, batching, or additional architecture was added.
- `clr-core` owns only the architecture-neutral context-limit error.
- `clr-storage` retains generic expert loading and cache policy.
- `clr-qwen3-moe` owns Qwen cache layout, attention state, and generation.
- `clr-cli` parses options and composes public APIs; it contains no tensor or
  inference implementation.
- ADRs 0009 and 0010 enumerate every public type, function, method, error
  variant, and tiny-fixture helper added by M3. Existing stateless forward APIs
  are unchanged.

## Known limitations

- Generation is proven only on the frozen tiny F32 Qwen3-MoE fixture.
- The CLI accepts token IDs directly and does not integrate a tokenizer.
- The KV cache is contiguous, fixed-capacity, and F32; no paging, sliding
  window, quantization, or batching is implemented.
- Streaming experts are still decoded into temporary F32 vectors on a miss.
- Full Qwen3-30B-A3B artifact conversion and correctness are M4 work.
- No throughput claim or production-readiness claim is made.

## Milestone decision

All M3 correctness, deterministic generation, context, cache-accounting,
resident/streaming equivalence, bounded-memory, CLI, and verification criteria
pass. M3 is closed. Work may proceed to M4.1 on `milestone/m4-full-qwen3`,
beginning with M4.1-01.
